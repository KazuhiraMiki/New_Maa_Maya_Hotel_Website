About

Hi, Nice to Meet You.

We are known as New Maa Maya Hotel, located in Silpasmiti, Jalpaiguri. A Bengali Cuisine specific restaurant. Started from 2018.

Back in November 2018

we decided to embark on a new journey by transforming our wooden shop and furniture business into a small Bengali homemade lunch restaurant. With no fancy setup, it was a humble effort driven by love for food and service. At that time, my parents managed everything—cooking and serving, ensuring every meal felt like home.

Our customers quickly embraced our cuisine, showering us with heartfelt reviews that motivated us to keep improving. Over time, we added many new dishes to our menu, each one loved and cherished by our patrons. We’ve always prioritized providing fresh, hygienic, and delicious food that feels just like a home-cooked meal.

Step by step, we grew together with our customers' support, and now we’re proud to celebrate over six wonderful years. Thank you for letting us be a part of your meals, and we look forward to serving you even better in the future!
